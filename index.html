About My Hometown – Dhaka

Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, is one of the most vibrant and densely populated cities in South Asia. Known as the city of mosques, rickshaws, and rich culture, Dhaka blends tradition with modernity in fascinating ways. From the historic Lalbagh Fort to the bustling streets of New Market, the city offers a unique mix of history, heritage, and daily hustle.

Dhaka is the economic and cultural heart of the country, home to major universities, garment industries, and a lively food scene. Despite its chaotic traffic and urban challenges, the people of Dhaka are known for their warmth, hospitality, and resilience. Whether it’s the aroma of biryani from Old Dhaka or the colorful festivals like Pohela Boishakh, Dhaka is always full of life and energy.
